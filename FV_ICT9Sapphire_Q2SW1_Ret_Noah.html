Dog Breeds
Dobermann Originated in Germany in the 19th Century. They have a sleek coat, athletic build, and regal appearance, this pup looks like an aristocrat. They’re highly energetic as well.
Pitbull They are a medium-size dog with a thick-set, low-slung body. They have a distinct short-muzzled head.
Shih Tzu From Tibet. Known for their very long and always growing coat and floppy ears.
